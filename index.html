Rajan's story

Learning digital skills made finding work easy!

When taxi driver Rajan, 50, moved from London to Oldham in Manchester, he struggled to find work. After being unemployed for almost three years, he decided to attend a Get Online Week session where he discovered our Digital Literacy website and developed his skills, helping him to finally secure a job.
